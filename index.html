About National Music Vision

National Music Vision (NMV) is India’s leading platform empowering independent artists, creators, and labels with all-in-one music services — from video production and channel management to OTT distribution and global promotion.

With our verified CMS/MCN status, trusted industry partnerships, and expert team, we help you scale your music brand across platforms. Whether you're a solo artist or a growing label, NMV is your partner in digital success.
